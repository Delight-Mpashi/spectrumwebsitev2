Account Questions

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by visiting our website and clicking on the "Sign Up" button. You'll need to provide some basic information including your name, email address, and phone number. After verification, your account will be ready to use.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password. If you don't see the email, please check your spam folder.

Loan Questions

What are the requirements to apply for a loan?

To apply for a loan, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Have a valid government-issued ID
  • Provide proof of income
  • Have an active bank account
  • Meet our creditworthiness criteria

How long does loan approval take?

For complete applications with all required documents, approval typically takes 1-2 business days. Some products may offer instant approval for pre-qualified customers.

Security Questions

How do you protect my personal information?

We use bank-level encryption and security measures to protect your data. All information is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel for legitimate business purposes. We comply with all applicable data protection laws including Zambia's Data Protection Act 2021.
